About Us

Buchi Hospital in Lagos state has been taking care of generations of Lagos state and South-west region residents. We are a full-service, acute care hospital with a 24/7 emergency department and nationally recognized patient safety. In fact, we're ranked among the top 5% in the nation for Patient Safety Excellence (2018-2022), top 5% in the nation for Pulmonary Care (2020-2022) and top 10% for GI Care (2020-2022) by Healthgrades.

Buchi Hospital in Lagos state is centrally located at Ojuelegba, Surulere between Domino's pizza and Mobil filling station. We are easily accessible from Yaba, Ikeja and Apapa. In addition to serving our community with a paramedic-receiving emergency department, our 420-bed hospital offers a wide range of inpatient and outpatient programs and services, including:

  • Behavioral health and chemical dependency programs
  • Center of Excellence weight-loss surgery program
  • Ears, nose and throat program
  • Emergency care service
  • General and specialized surgery
  • Intensive Care Unit
  • Orthopedics and spine programs
  • Radiology and imaging service
  • Rehabilitation service

Our behavioral health unit and Miracles dependency programs provide a much-needed safety net for Lagos state and surrounding communities.

Quality, Accessible Care
Our primary focus is on the complete care of the patient—in a healing environment, with compassionate care and a collaborative team approach to ensure the best outcomes for our patients. You can be confident you are being taken care of by expert medical and support teams.

Buchi Hospital is accredited by The Joint Commission. The Gold Seal of Approval® is a symbol of quality that reflects our commitment to providing safe and effective patient care.

We also are nationally recognized in cardiac, esophageal, pulmonary care, as well as patient safety by Healthgrades. Clinical quality varies between hospitals and consumers can use this Healthgrades information to assess where to receive care.

For analysis, Healthgrades evaluated approximately 45 million Medicare inpatient records for almost 4,500 hospitals nationwide to assess hospital performance in 31 common conditions and procedures to evaluate outcomes and identify 5-Star Recipients.

We have committed to become a high reliability organization (HRO), which means our goal is to commit zero harm and embrace the purposeful work we do in healthcare. We look forward to our continual transformation as we care for our community.

Community Legacy
From the day it opened as an eight-bed clinic in 2000, Buchi Hospital has dedicated itself to providing quality care to the culturally diverse populations of Lagos state and the nation.

Now at 420 beds, our general acute care hospital utilizes many of the latest medical treatments and technologies. The hospital offers a wide range of inpatient and outpatient services and programs.
