Services

At Buch Hospital, we offer expert-care services and a community of facilities, conveniently located on our campus and within close proximity. These services and centers provide our patients with special access to care, staffed by a multidisciplinary team of highly trained specialists and fully equipped with advanced technology. Our patient and family-centered approach ensures you and your family are given the treatment and support needed for a variety of conditions and recovery.

  • Emergency care service
  • Intensive care unit
  • Radiology and imaging service
  • Rehabilitation service
OUR MISSION

To provide healthcare services in a fiscally responsible manner which contribute to the physical, psychological, social and spiritual well being of the patients and community which it serves.

OUR VALUES

Compassion, Dignity, Justice, Excellence, Integrity.
